Introduction¶

1Q1D (One Question in one Day) is a platform on which we are discussing programming concepts and try to answer questions which are generally asked in interviews. 1Q1D basically refers to C/C++ programming, Embedded Systems and Linux OS concepts and try to explain them in brief.
